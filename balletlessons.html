Young Dancers: Ages 2-6

Beginner dancers at young ages! This age group will start by learning how to move their body and include some jazz as well as ballet to help expand their ability to move at a young age.

Price: $25 per month, per child (First class is free)

Sign Up

Middle Dancers: Ages 7-13

Beginner to mid level dancers at older ages. This age group will start to learn about the different muscles in the body and how to take care of themselves while they dance to avoid injury. More technical ballet will be taught such as starting at the barre as well as some things on the floor.

Price: $35 per month, per person

Sign Up

High to Adult Dancers: Ages 14+

Also tailored for beginner to mid dancers who are teens all the way up until adulthood. This will be more technical floor routines as well as technical barre moves. Some pre-pointe taught, but no pointe offered at this time. Pointe will be coming soon!

Price: $45 per month, per person

Sign Up

Required Equipment

It is required to have a black leotard, pink ballet tights, and ballet shoes. For some of the younger ages, they can wear leggings. Upper students are not advised to wear leggings, but accomadations can be made if discussed in advance.
